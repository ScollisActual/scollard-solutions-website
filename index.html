About

Real Training. Real Background.

Scollard Solutions was built on a simple premise: the skills developed over years in Special Forces don't stop being useful when you take off the uniform. They get applied differently.

Ryan Scollard is a Special Forces Intelligence Sergeant (18F) with experience in all-source intelligence analysis, intelligence preparation of the battlefield, and operational security across multiple theaters. That background now goes toward teaching civilians how to think about their own security.

This isn't fearmongering. It's practical training that makes you harder to target, smarter about your environment, and more capable of protecting the people around you.
