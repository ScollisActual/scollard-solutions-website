A Zero Hour engagement is the culminating event of a full preparedness program — a live, multi-day immersive scenario that puts your planning, your people, and your resources under real pressure. Not simulated pressure. The kind that reveals gaps that months of preparation missed.

It is designed for individuals, families, and organizations who have already invested seriously in preparedness and want an honest answer to one question: does it actually hold?

About

Built for Complex Problems.

Scollard Solutions was built on one idea: that the kind of rigorous, layered planning and preparation most people never encounter should be accessible to the individuals, families, and organizations that need it most.

The methodology draws on deep experience in intelligence analysis, systems engineering, technical fabrication, and operational planning — applied to the real environments clients actually live and work in.

The standard for what good preparation looks like comes from environments where the cost of being wrong is final. That standard doesn't lower when the stakes appear smaller.
