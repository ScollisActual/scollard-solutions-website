The Army Special Forces Alphabet
By Ryan Scollard

An alphabet book unlike any other, each letter introduces children to the world of Army Special Forces through age-appropriate concepts, equipment, and values. Built on the belief that the lessons of the Special Forces community, discipline, preparation, teamwork, and resilience, belong in every household from an early age.

Written by an 18-year Special Forces veteran who wanted to share the ethos of the SOF community with the next generation, in a format that starts at A and ends at Z, but teaches a whole lot more in between.
